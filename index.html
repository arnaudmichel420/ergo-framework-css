the team

Cmdr. Elena Voss

Chief Flight Commander

With over 22 years of experience in aerospace and three missions to the International Space Station, Commander Voss leads every Orbit & Go expedition with unmatched precision. Her vision: making space travel as safe and accessible as a transatlantic flight.

Before joining Orbit & Go, she served as a senior test pilot for ESA and contributed to the development of next-generation life-support systems. A decorated engineer and a natural leader, Commander Voss has trained over 60 crew members and remains the most trusted name in commercial deep-space operations.

About

Founded in 2021, Orbit & Go is the first commercial space agency to offer fully certified passenger flights beyond Earth's orbit. We believe the cosmos should not be reserved for astronauts alone. Our mission is to open the final frontier to everyone — safely, responsibly, and unforgettably.

Headquartered in Cape Canaveral with launch operations in Baikonur and Kourou, we operate under the strictest international safety standards. Each journey is meticulously planned by a team of world-class engineers, physicians, and mission specialists dedicated to giving you the experience of a lifetime — beyond the atmosphere.

  • Over 340 passengers safely flown since 2023
  • 3 certified deep-space vessels in active service
  • Destinations: Low Earth Orbit, Moon & Mars
